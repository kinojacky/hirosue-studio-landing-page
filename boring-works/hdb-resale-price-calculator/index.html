About HDB Resale Price Calculator
For PR First-Time Buyers:
  • Bank loan: Maximum 75% of property value/price
  • Down payment: 25% of property value
    • Minimum 5% must be in cash
    • Remaining 20% can be paid using CPF or cash
  • ABSD (Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty): 5% for first-time PR buyers
  • Agent fee is typically 1-2% of property price (negotiable)

Note: Must be a PR for at least 3 years before purchasing

About Total Debt Servicing Ratio (TDSR)

TDSR is a framework that limits your total monthly debt obligations to 55% of your gross monthly income.

Includes:
  • Housing loan payments
  • Car loans
  • Personal loans
  • Credit card debts
  • Student loans
  • Any other debt payments

For example, if your monthly income is $5,000, your total monthly debt payments cannot exceed $2,750.

Note: You can include combined household income if applying as a family

About Mortgage Servicing Ratio (MSR)

MSR is a restriction specific to HDB property loans that limits your monthly mortgage payment to 30% of your gross monthly income.

Key Points:
  • Applies only to HDB loans
  • Based on gross monthly income
  • Considers only the housing loan payment
  • Can use combined income for family applications

For example, if your monthly income is $5,000, your monthly housing loan payment cannot exceed $1,500.

Note: Both TDSR and MSR limits must be met to qualify for the loan
